# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Dog owners shopping for cheerful everyday gear: beds, leashes, toys, grooming tools, feeding accessories, and small walking essentials. They are browsing on mobile first, often comparing quality, mood, price, and giftability before deciding what to buy.

## Product Purpose

Pawberry Market is a static, launch-ready storefront for a warm dog supplies brand. The site should make products feel cared-for and easy to understand, collect early interest before payments are connected, and be ready for Vercel deployment.

## Brand Personality

Warm, tidy, and playful. The brand should feel like a sunny neighborhood pet shop with careful merchandising rather than a generic marketplace.

## Anti-references

Avoid Amazon-style density, sterile veterinary branding, generic pastel SaaS landing pages, template card grids, and dark luxury pet boutique aesthetics.

## Design Principles

Show the products in use instead of explaining the brand at length.
Make the first screen immediately shoppable, not a marketing splash page.
Use cute details with restraint so the store still feels trustworthy.
Keep mobile browsing quick: clear categories, prices, and short product copy.
Prepare for future checkout without pretending payment is live today.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, descriptive alt text, reduced-motion support, and readable type for long product descriptions.
